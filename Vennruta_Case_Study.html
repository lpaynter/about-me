THE PROBLEM
Simplify decisions of experience planning for single and group travelers.
THE SOLUTION
VENNRUTA is the mobile app to help travelers make quick and easy work of planning their itineraries. Sync preferences with your companions and make choices from what you all love to do, no matter where you are. The app keeps your shared itinerary and notifies you and your companions with all the details and maps to get you all where you want to be - together.

User Research

After creating questions and conducting 7 interviews revolving around frustrations around travel, I synthesized data. I did this using various methods like sorting interview insights, affinity and empathy mapping, and creating personas. I love this space of the process. I fing it to be very helpful when writing user guides for marketing purposes.

Definition and Ideation.

This is an exciting phase. I love to brainstorm and build on ideas with others. Creating user insight statements and value propositions, are so helpful with getting the team -if there is one, on the same page.

Prototyping and test test test

I'm a user centric designer with a print and web background that includes brand identity and guidelines and a host of other means of communication. I'm jumping into the UX world.
